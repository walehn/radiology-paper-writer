## Radiology (RSNA)

### General Information
- **Publisher:** Radiological Society of North America
- **Impact Factor:** ~29 (2024)
- **Acceptance Rate:** ~15%
- **Review Time:** 4-6 weeks

### Word Limits

| Article Type | Abstract | Main Text | References | Tables/Figures |
|--------------|----------|-----------|------------|----------------|
| Original Research | 250 | 4500 | 35-40 | 6 total |
| Technical Development | 250 | 4000 | 30 | 6 total |
| Review Article | 250 | 6000 | 100 | 8 total |

### Abstract Requirements
- **Format:** Structured (Purpose, Materials and Methods, Results, Conclusion)
- **No abbreviations** (except standard units)
- **No citations** in abstract

### Formatting Preferences

**Statistical Notation:**
```
P < .05 (capital P, no leading zero)
95% CI (always include)
AUC = 0.92 (not 92%)
Sensitivity, 92% (comma, not colon)
```

**Numbers:**
- Spell out 1-9 (except measurements)
- Use numerals for 10+
- Use SI units when possible

**Structure Preferences:**
- Methods subsections required
- Statistical analysis as last Methods subsection
- Results should mirror Methods organization
- Discussion should not exceed 1/3 of manuscript

### AI Study Requirements
- CLAIM checklist required
- Code availability statement
- Training/validation/test split description
- External validation encouraged

### Key Points Section
- Required for all articles
- 3-5 bullet points
- No abbreviations

## European Radiology

- **Publisher:** European Society of Radiology / Springer
- **Impact Factor:** ~7 (2024)
- **Acceptance Rate:** ~20%
- **Review Time:** 3-5 weeks

### Word Limits

| Article Type | Abstract | Main Text | References | Tables/Figures |
|--------------|----------|-----------|------------|----------------|
| Original Article | 250 | 3500 | 30 | 5 total |
| Review | 250 | 5000 | 80 | 6 total |
| Technical Note | 150 | 2000 | 15 | 3 total |

### Abstract Requirements
- **Format:** Structured (Objectives, Methods, Results, Conclusions)
- Maximum 250 words

### Unique Requirements
- **Key Points:** 3-5 bullet points required
- **Graphical Abstract:** Encouraged
- **ESR Reporting Guidelines:** Reference when applicable
- **ORCID:** Required for all authors

### Formatting Preferences

**Statistical Notation:**
```
p < 0.05 (lowercase p, leading zero)
95% confidence interval
AUC 0.92
```

**Numbers:**
- Numerals for all measurements
- Spell out numbers at sentence start

## AJR (American Journal of Roentgenology)

### General Information
- **Publisher:** American Roentgen Ray Society
- **Impact Factor:** ~5 (2024)
- **Acceptance Rate:** ~25%
- **Review Time:** 3-4 weeks

### Word Limits

| Article Type | Abstract | Main Text | References | Tables/Figures |
|--------------|----------|-----------|------------|----------------|
| Original Research | 250 | 3000 | 30 | 5 total |
| Pictorial Essay | 100 | 3000 | 20 | 15-20 |
| Technical Innovation | 150 | 2500 | 20 | 4 total |

### Abstract Requirements
- **Format:** Structured (Background, Objective, Methods, Results, Conclusion)
- Maximum 250 words

### Style Preferences
- **Active voice** strongly preferred
- Avoid "we" at sentence start
- Concise writing emphasized

### Formatting Preferences

**Measurements:**
```
3.2 cm (range, 1.0-5.4 cm)
Mean ± SD format
p values to 3 decimal places
```

**References:**
- Vancouver style
- Abbreviated journal names

## Radiology: Artificial Intelligence

### General Information
- **Publisher:** RSNA
- **Impact Factor:** ~8 (2024)
- **Focus:** AI/ML in radiology
- **Review Time:** 4-6 weeks

### Word Limits

| Article Type | Abstract | Main Text | References |
|--------------|----------|-----------|------------|
| Original Research | 300 | 5000 | 50 |
| Technical Note | 200 | 3000 | 30 |
| Review | 300 | 6000 | 100 |

### Abstract Requirements
- **Format:** Structured
- 300 words maximum
- Include key performance metrics

### Mandatory Requirements
- **CLAIM checklist** submission
- **Code availability** statement
- **Data availability** statement
- **External validation** strongly encouraged

### AI-Specific Formatting

**Model Performance:**
```
AUC, 0.92 (95% CI: 0.89-0.95)
Sensitivity, 85% at 90% specificity
Operating threshold: 0.30
```

**Training Details:**
- Architecture description
- Training data source and size
- Validation strategy
- Hardware specifications

## JMRI (Journal of Magnetic Resonance Imaging)

### General Information
- **Publisher:** International Society for Magnetic Resonance in Medicine / Wiley
- **Impact Factor:** ~4 (2024)
- **Focus:** MRI research
- **Review Time:** 4-6 weeks

### Word Limits

| Article Type | Abstract | Main Text | References |
|--------------|----------|-----------|------------|
| Original Research | 300 | 4000 | 40 |
| Technical Note | 150 | 2500 | 20 |
| Review | 300 | 5000 | 80 |

### MRI-Specific Requirements
- **Sequence parameters:** Must include TR, TE, flip angle, FOV, matrix, slice thickness
- **Field strength:** Always specified
- **Coil information:** Required
- **Vendor:** Scanner manufacturer stated

### Formatting Preferences

**MRI Parameters:**
```
T1-weighted imaging (TR/TE, 500/10 ms; flip angle, 90°;
FOV, 300 × 300 mm; matrix, 256 × 256; slice thickness, 5 mm)
```
